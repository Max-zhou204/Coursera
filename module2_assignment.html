Our menu

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a beloved Indian dish known for its creamy, rich sauce and tender pieces of chicken. This delightful recipe combines aromatic spices like garam masala and turmeric with tomato puree and cream, creating a comforting and flavorful meal often served with rice or naan.

Meatball Pasta

Meatball Pasta is a classic Italian-American comfort food that combines succulent meatballs with al dente pasta drenched in a robust marinara sauce. The meatballs, made from ground meat, herbs, and breadcrumbs, are simmered in the sauce, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully.

Firegrill Sushi

Firegrill Sushi introduces a delightful twist to sushi by incorporating grilled ingredients. This variant might feature grilled seafood, paired with vegetables and seasoned rice, all wrapped in nori. It's a fusion that brings a depth to the fresh, clean flavors of classic sushi.
